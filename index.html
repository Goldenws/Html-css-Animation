I ♥ Macaron Salad, its the best.

Ingredients

Ingriedients Amount
sausage 800g
pikcles 400g
Macarons 500g
Mayo Optional
Sour Cream 250g
Mustard Optional

Recipe

  • Chop All the ingriedients into small pieces.
  • Put the Ingredients into a bowl.
  • Mix it until all Ingredients will be aproximately on all levels of salad.
  • Add Mayo, sour Cream and Mustard.
  • Mix it till the the sauces will cover all tthe Ingredients.
  • Enjoy!
